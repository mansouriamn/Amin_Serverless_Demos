				Amazon Cognito

1- Open Cognito
2- Click Manage User pools
3- Click "Create User Pool" and call it "App1" and click "Review Defaults"
4- Click Create Pool
5- Click on "App Client" 
6- Click "Add an App client" and click "Create App Client"
7- Click on "App Integrations" to see how this app will integrate with the user pool.
8- Enable the "Cognito User Pool" by check marking iy
